PARKOUR

Parkour can be defined as a diciplinary sport which involves the art of moving from point A to B in the most elaborate and fluid way possible.

Popular in most European Countries, it is a sport that requires skill, agility and practice.

It was founded by David Belle, a French actor, stunt performer and former member of the Yamazakis.
